Some Quick Points

  • Safety is very important while Machining.(use mask and Gogle)
  • Don't Move tool after completation or stopping of job rasie tool by upward from z axix then move ( Begineer usually make this mistake )
  • You can once check your tool path by keeping z axis above the material
  • Always check toolpath and profile sequence before starting your cutting
  • have a check for T bone if you are doing somthing on pressfit
  • Very Important : Check for tool path thr should be no clamps or metal screw or bolt. ( It can create a accident and its going to hit 5000+ rpm tool)
  • Plastic clamps are used to avoid accident
  • At Fablab CEPT : Our Instructur has tied Key ( Powering On) with Drillbit changing tool so Machine is always off condition while changing
  • Drill bit hold should be proper otherwise thr is chance of breaking it as vibration will be more if lengh
  • Always have checklist of safety for yourself and machine both. to avoid delays and accident
